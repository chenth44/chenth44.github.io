# Hessian Matrix  
  
$$  
(\mathbf{H}_ {f})_ {ij} = \frac{ \partial^2 f }{ \partial x_ {i} \partial x_ {j} }   
$$  
The Hessian matrix is the transpose of the Jacobian of the gradient.  
$$  
\mathbf{H}(f(\mathbf{x})) = \mathbf{J}(\nabla f(\mathbf{x}))^{\mathrm{T}}.  
$$
